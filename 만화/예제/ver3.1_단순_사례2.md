**Style Setting:** - 4-panel square comic strip, Zen-minimalist hand-drawn style (Ref: image_22.png).
- Loose, organic ink lines with muted, earthy tones on beige paper texture. 
- **NO HUMANS.** Every object is personified.
- High negative space (여백의 미).

**Character Design (The Hero):** - "Stone-Buddha MDF" (돌부처 MDF): A perfectly sharp, thick rectangular block. 
- Face: Two tiny dot eyes and one flat horizontal line (deadpan expression). 
- Personality: Unfazed, stoic, and stable.

**Panel 1: Intro**
- **Visual:** The Hero stands alone in a clean, empty space.
- **Text (Handwritten Korean):** "난 고밀도 MDF. 치수 안정성 그 자체."
- **Effect:** A very faint, warm orange glow around the Hero.

**Panel 2: Chaos (The Nightmare)**
- **Visual:** A "Regular MDF" character is bloated like a round balloon, covered in cracks (cracking sounds: '쩌적!'). It is sweating (blue wavy lines). Mean-looking blue water droplets are poking it.
- **Text (Handwritten Korean):** "습기! 으악! 뚱뚱해져!"

**Panel 3: Peace (The Shield)**
- **Visual:** The Hero is perfectly fitted between two wall-frames. The mean water droplets are now tiny and bouncing off the Hero's flat surface harmlessly. The Hero remains deadpan.
- **Text (Handwritten Korean):** "이제 됐습니다."

**Panel 4: Punchline**
- **Visual:** Close-up of the Hero’s flat, stable edge. An arrow points to the edge.
- **Text (Label):** "물 먹어도 안 뚱뚱함."
- **Text (Bottom Center):** "현장에서 드라마는 필요 없습니다."

# 5. AI 생성 프롬프트 (Flow 입력용)

Create a cinematic 30-second trailer introducing a building materials distribution company that supplies everything needed for construction, from structural materials to doors and windows.

The video begins in a real construction site and gradually transitions into a futuristic architectural universe made entirely of building materials.

Scene 1 — A large construction site at sunrise with cranes, trucks delivering plywood, drywall boards, insulation panels, doors and window frames. Workers prepare materials while dust particles float in sunlight. The camera slowly moves forward between the stacks.

Scene 2 — The camera enters the stacked materials. The world transforms into a surreal architectural environment made entirely of building materials.

Scene 3 — Massive wooden beams and plywood structures rise upward forming structural frames. Drywall panels slide into place forming walls. Insulation boards glow with thermal energy filling the structure.

Scene 4 — Doors appear and open automatically between spaces. Modern window systems install into the structure and sunlight shines through the windows.

Scene 5 — The completed building expands outward forming multiple buildings. A futuristic city made of construction materials emerges.

Scene 6 — The camera rises above the architectural city. Dramatic cinematic lighting appears.

Text appears:

Speed  
Accuracy  
Quality  
Price  

Final message:

The No.1 Building Materials Distribution Leader

Style: epic cinematic architecture, hyper realistic materials, futuristic building material world, dramatic lighting, smooth camera movement.
